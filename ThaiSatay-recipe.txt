Satay Marinade:
1/4 c. minced lemongrass , fresh or frozen
2 shallots OR 1/4 onion, sliced
4 cloves garlic
1-2 fresh red chilies, sliced, OR 1/2 tsp. to 1 tsp. cayenne pepper, to taste
1 thumb-size piece galangal OR ginger, thinly sliced
1/2 tsp. turmeric
2 tbsp. ground coriander
2 tsp. cumin
3 tbsp. dark soy sauce (available at Asian food stores)
3 tbsp. fish sauce
6 tbsp. brown sugar (you need all of this)
2 tbsp. vegetable oil
